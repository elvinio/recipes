# Shrimp Tacos with Mango Salsa

> Serves 4 | 25 minutes | Mexican | dinner

## Ingredients

- 600 g large raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 12 small corn tortillas (15 cm diameter)
- 1 large ripe mango, peeled and diced into 1 cm cubes (approx. 300 g flesh)
- 1 medium red onion, finely diced (approx. 150 g)
- 1 jalapeño, finely diced (seeds in for heat)
- 30 g fresh cilantro, leaves and fine stems chopped
- 2 limes, juiced (approx. 60 mL), plus wedges to serve
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp cayenne pepper
- 2 tbsp neutral oil
- 150 g shredded purple cabbage
- 80 mL (⅓ cup) sour cream or Mexican crema
- 1 tbsp hot sauce (such as Cholula)
- Salt to taste

## Steps

1. Make the mango salsa: combine diced mango, half the red onion, jalapeño, cilantro, and half the lime juice in a bowl. Season with salt and set aside at room temperature to macerate while you prepare the shrimp.
2. Combine smoked paprika, cumin, garlic powder, cayenne, and a generous pinch of salt in a bowl. Add shrimp and toss to coat evenly.
3. Stir together the sour cream and hot sauce in a small bowl. Set aside.
4. Warm the corn tortillas directly over a gas burner or in a dry skillet over high heat for 20–30 seconds per side until lightly charred in spots. Wrap in a clean tea towel to keep warm.
5. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat until shimmering. Add shrimp in a single layer — work in two batches if needed to avoid steaming. Cook for 90 seconds per side until the shrimp are pink, curled, and slightly caramelised at the edges.
6. Squeeze the remaining lime juice over the shrimp and toss to coat. Remove from heat immediately.
7. To assemble: lay two tortillas per person. Spread a little chipotle crema on each, top with shredded cabbage, 3–4 shrimp, and a generous spoonful of mango salsa.
8. Serve immediately with extra lime wedges on the side.

## Special Notice

> Cook shrimp on the highest heat your stove allows and never overcrowd the pan — this is key to getting a proper sear rather than a stew. The mango salsa keeps for up to 1 day refrigerated but is best made fresh. For a smokier flavour, add ½ tsp chipotle powder to the shrimp spice mix.
