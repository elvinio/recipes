# Prawn and Feta Baked Dish

> Serves 4 | 40 minutes | Mediterranean | dinner

## Ingredients

- 600 g large raw prawns, peeled and deveined, tails on
- 200 g feta cheese, broken into large chunks
- 500 g cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1 tsp chilli flakes
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp sweet paprika
- 80 mL dry white wine
- 60 mL olive oil
- Zest of 1 lemon
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Fresh flat-leaf parsley, to serve
- Crusty bread, to serve

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 210°C. In a large baking dish (approximately 30×20 cm), combine cherry tomatoes, red onion, garlic, chilli flakes, oregano, paprika, lemon zest, white wine, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper and toss to combine.
2. Roast the tomato mixture for 18–20 minutes until the tomatoes have collapsed, caramelised at the edges, and released their juices.
3. Remove dish from oven. Nestle prawns into the tomato sauce in a single layer, pressing down so they are partially submerged.
4. Scatter feta chunks generously over the prawns and tomatoes.
5. Return to oven and bake for a further 10–12 minutes until prawns are pink and cooked through, and feta has softened and begun to colour.
6. Remove from oven and rest for 2 minutes. Scatter fresh parsley over the dish.
7. Serve immediately, directly from the baking dish, with plenty of crusty bread to soak up the tomato and feta juices.

## Special Notice

> Prawns cook very quickly in the oven — check at the 8-minute mark and remove as soon as they are pink all the way through to avoid rubbery texture. This dish is best served immediately and does not reheat as well due to the delicate nature of prawns.
