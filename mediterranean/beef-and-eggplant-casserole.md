# Beef and Eggplant Casserole

> Serves 4 | 55 minutes | Mediterranean | dinner

## Ingredients

- 500 g ground beef (or beef cut into small 2 cm cubes)
- 2 medium eggplants (about 700 g total), cut into 2 cm cubes
- 1 large yellow onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 400 g canned crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 120 mL beef stock or water
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp sweet paprika
- 1/2 tsp ground allspice
- 60 mL olive oil, divided
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 40 g grated Parmesan or kefalotyri, to finish
- Fresh flat-leaf parsley, to serve

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 210°C. Toss eggplant cubes with 3 tbsp olive oil, season generously with salt, and spread on a lined baking tray. Roast for 20 minutes, turning once, until golden and tender. Reduce oven to 190°C.
2. While eggplant roasts, heat remaining olive oil in a large oven-safe pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook for 4–5 minutes until softened.
3. Add garlic, cinnamon, oregano, paprika, and allspice. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Add beef and cook, breaking it up, for 6–8 minutes until browned and any liquid has evaporated.
5. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 1 minute. Add crushed tomatoes and stock. Season well with salt and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.
6. Fold roasted eggplant into the beef mixture gently. Transfer pot to the oven or pour into a deep baking dish.
7. Scatter grated cheese over the top. Bake at 190°C for 20–25 minutes until bubbling, slightly reduced, and golden on top.
8. Rest for 5 minutes before serving scattered with fresh parsley.

## Special Notice

> Roasting the eggplant separately before combining ensures it stays intact and doesn't become mushy in the casserole. This dish reheats excellently the next day as the flavours deepen overnight. Serve with a simple green salad and crusty bread.
