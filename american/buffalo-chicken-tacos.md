# Buffalo Chicken Tacos

> Serves 4 | 25 minutes | American | dinner

## Ingredients

- 600 g boneless skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
- 8 small flour tortillas (15 cm)
- 120 mL (1/2 cup) Frank's RedHot sauce (or preferred hot sauce)
- 3 tbsp unsalted butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 100 g blue cheese crumbles
- 2 celery stalks, thinly sliced
- 1 medium carrot, grated
- 120 mL (1/2 cup) sour cream
- 2 tbsp ranch dressing
- 30 g fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
- 1 lime, cut into wedges

## Steps

1. Season chicken slices with garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, and salt.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken in a single layer (work in batches if needed) and cook for 3–4 minutes per side until cooked through and golden. Remove and set aside.
3. In the same skillet over low heat, melt butter. Add hot sauce and stir to combine. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and toss to coat every piece in the buffalo sauce. Cook for 1 minute, then remove from heat.
4. Warm tortillas directly over a medium flame on the induction stove or in a dry skillet for 20–30 seconds per side.
5. Whisk sour cream and ranch dressing together in a small bowl.
6. Assemble tacos: spread a spoonful of the sour cream ranch on each tortilla. Top with buffalo chicken, blue cheese crumbles, sliced celery, grated carrot, and cilantro.
7. Serve immediately with lime wedges for squeezing over.

## Special Notice

> The heat level depends entirely on your hot sauce — Frank's RedHot gives a classic medium-hot buffalo flavor, but you can add 1 tsp of cayenne to the sauce for extra fire. Blue cheese is traditional for buffalo flavor; if you prefer, substitute crumbled feta or a dollop of extra ranch. Tortillas are best warmed right before assembly to keep them pliable and prevent cracking.
