# Chicken Caesar Wrap

> Serves 4 | 10 minutes | American | lunch

## Ingredients

- 500 g rotisserie chicken, shredded (breast and thigh meat)
- 4 large flour tortillas (25 cm)
- 120 g romaine lettuce, thinly shredded
- 80 g (1/3 cup) Caesar dressing
- 40 g Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
- 60 g croutons, lightly crushed
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
- 8 anchovy fillets, roughly chopped (optional)
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

## Steps

1. In a large bowl, combine shredded chicken, Caesar dressing, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, and black pepper. Toss to coat evenly. Taste and adjust seasoning.
2. Add the shredded romaine and toss gently — just enough to lightly dress the lettuce without wilting it.
3. Warm tortillas briefly: microwave each one for 15–20 seconds, or heat in a dry pan for 20 seconds per side, to make them pliable.
4. Lay each tortilla flat. Scatter a tablespoon of crushed croutons in the center of each. Spoon a quarter of the chicken and lettuce mixture down the center, leaving 5 cm clear at each end.
5. Sprinkle grated Parmesan and anchovy fillets (if using) over the filling.
6. Fold the short ends in, then roll the tortilla tightly from one long side to the other to form a compact wrap. Slice diagonally in half and serve immediately.

## Special Notice

> Assemble wraps immediately before eating — once dressed, the lettuce will wilt and the tortilla will soften within 30 minutes. For packed lunches, store the chicken mixture and lettuce separately and assemble on site. Rotisserie chicken is the key time-saver here; alternatively, use any leftover cooked chicken breast.
