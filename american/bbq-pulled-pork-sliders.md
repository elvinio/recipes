# BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

> Serves 4 | 55 minutes | American | dinner

## Ingredients

- 900 g boneless pork shoulder, cut into 5 cm chunks
- 8 slider buns
- 240 mL (1 cup) BBQ sauce (store-bought or homemade)
- 120 mL (1/2 cup) apple cider vinegar
- 120 mL (1/2 cup) chicken broth
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 200 g coleslaw mix
- 3 tbsp mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar (for slaw)

## Steps

1. In a small bowl, combine smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, brown sugar, salt, and pepper. Rub the spice mixture all over the pork chunks.
2. Pour chicken broth and 120 mL apple cider vinegar into the electric pressure cooker insert. Add the seasoned pork.
3. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 35 minutes. Allow a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.
4. While the pork cooks, prepare the slaw: toss coleslaw mix with mayonnaise and 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar in a bowl. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper, then refrigerate.
5. Transfer the cooked pork to a large bowl. Use two forks to shred the meat, discarding any large fat pieces. Stir in BBQ sauce and a few tablespoons of the cooking liquid to keep the pork moist.
6. Toast the slider buns cut-side down in a dry skillet over medium heat for 1–2 minutes.
7. Pile the pulled pork onto the bottom buns, top with a generous spoonful of slaw, and close with the top buns. Serve immediately.

## Special Notice

> Pulled pork stores very well — refrigerate in an airtight container with its cooking liquid for up to 4 days, or freeze for up to 3 months. Reheat gently on the stovetop with a splash of broth. The slaw should be made no more than 1 hour before serving to keep its crunch.
